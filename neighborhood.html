Powder Springs, GA

Welcome to my neighborhood!!!

Powder Springs, situated in Cobb County, Georgia, USA, is a hub for outdoor family activities and entertainment, situated just 25 miles to the northwest of Atlanta. The town's picturesque historic downtown and Towne Square regularly host events, concerts, and shopping experiences, and are linked to the renowned Silver Comet Trail. Powder Springs offers several other attractions, such as the Southern Quilt Trail, Seven Springs Museum, the highly-regarded Cobb County BMX park, integrated bike and pedestrian paths, and numerous recreational parks, including the recently opened Seven Springs Water Park.
